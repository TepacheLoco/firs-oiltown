FIRS OilTown

A single-economy FIRS fork for OpenTTD. Everything revolves around oil and gas: pump it, crack it, ship it, burn it, and feed the cycle with engineering supplies.

Install Supply chain GitHub

What OilTown is

OilTown is a temperate-climate, single-economy fork of FIRS. It keeps FIRS's upstream industries that make sense for an oil world and adds a full petrochemistry chain on top: cracking, plastics, fertiliser, bitumen, lubricants, fracking, treated water, and export trading.

The hook: a five-tier chain where every output feeds something further downstream, and a feedback loop in which the engineering supplies you manufacture are what boost your upstream extraction.

The supply chain

Five tiers. Arrows flow downstream; the engineering supplies loop runs the other way.

1

Extraction

  • Offshore Oil Rig→ crude oil (+ passengers byproduct)
  • Onshore Oil Wells→ crude oil (+ small raw gas)
  • Natural Gas Well→ raw gas
  • Coal Mine→ coal
  • Desalination Plantcoastal→ treated water
  • Fracking Wellrequires fracking fluid→ raw gas + condensate
2

Primary processing

  • Oil Refinerycrude (+ optional condensate) → light oil + heavy oil + naphtha + refinery gas
  • Gas Processing Plantraw gas → LNG + LPG + sulphur
  • Coking Plantcoal → coke + coal tar
3

Petrochemistry

  • Cracking Plantnaphtha + refinery gas + coal tar → ethylene + chemicals
  • Chemical Plantethylene + sulphur + treated water → chemicals
  • Fracking Fluid Plantchemicals + treated water → fracking fluid
4

Processing & production

  • Lubricants Plantheavy oil + LNG → lubricants
  • Bitumen Plantheavy oil + coke → bitumen
  • Plastics Plantethylene + chemicals → plastics
  • Fertiliser PlantLNG + sulphur + chemicals → fertiliser
  • Petroleum Refinerylight oil + chemicals + lubricants → petroleum fuels
  • Engineering Supplies Plantlubricants + bitumen + coke + ethylene + coal tar → engineering supplies

Sinks

  • Fuel Terminalcoastalaccepts crude / light / heavy oil, petrol, LNG, LPG
  • Trading Portcoastalchemicals + plastics + fertiliser → food (bonus when all 3 supplied)
  • Power Stationaccepts coal / heavy oil / LNG
  • Water Towerin-townaccepts treated water — boosts town happiness

The feedback loops

Engineering supplies boost extraction

Delivering engineering supplies to any tier-1 extractive (coal mine, oil wells, natural gas well, oil rig) boosts its output by roughly +40%. The supplies themselves come out of tier 4, which means every barrel of crude and tonne of coal you produce feeds back into more crude and more coal down the line.

Fracking needs fluid

Unlike the other extractives, a fracking well requires fracking fluid deliveries to operate — no fluid, no gas. It rewards you for the long chain you built to make it (chemicals → fracking fluid → well): ~5× gas and ~1× condensate per unit of fluid delivered.

Treated water for towns

Treated water has the same town-delivery effect food has in other FIRS economies: haul it to a water tower and you'll see the usual growth / happiness bonus kick in.

Install

Published to BaNaNaS, OpenTTD's online content service — no manual file handling required.

  1. Open the online content browser

    From the OpenTTD main menu, click Check online content.

  2. Find the package

    Filter by NewGRF and search for FIRS Forked: Oil Town. Tick it and click Download.

  3. Enable it and start a new game

    Start a new game (temperate climate). In the NewGRF settings, enable FIRS Forked: Oil Town. The economy parameter is fixed to Oil Town — no configuration needed.

Prefer to build from source? Clone the GitHub repo and follow the build instructions in the README.
